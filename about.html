Who We Are

Studios By Ritumbhara offers comfortable and premium stay experiences designed for convenience and relaxation.

Located in Rajasthan, we aim to provide quality service and a seamless booking experience for our guests.

Today, we manage multiple properties across Jaipur and Alwar, serving travelers with professional hospitality and comfortable accommodations.

Founder

The visionary behind Studios By Ritumbhara. With a passion for hospitality and real estate, Shivam has transformed how guests experience short-term stays in Rajasthan.

His mission is simple: provide travelers with immaculate spaces that feel like home — only better. Every property under our management reflects his commitment to quality, cleanliness, and guest experience.
